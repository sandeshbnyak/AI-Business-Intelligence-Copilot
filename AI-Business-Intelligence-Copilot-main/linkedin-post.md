# 🚀 Exciting Project Reveal: AI Business Intelligence Copilot!

I’m thrilled to share my latest project—an AI-powered Business Intelligence (BI) tool that turns raw data into interactive dashboards in seconds. 📊🧠

Most BI tools require complex SQL or dragging cells. This platform lets you **just ask questions**.

### 🌟 Key Highlights:
- **Conversational Analytics:** Chat with your CSVs, Excel files, and even PDFs.
- **Root Cause Analysis (RCA):** Don't just see a drop—ask "Why did sales drop?" and let the AI find the specific drivers.
- **Smart KPI Engine:** Instantly generates bold, real-time KPI metrics for your most critical numbers.
- **Auto-Dashboards:** One query like "Build a revenue dashboard" creates a multi-view, responsive layout.
- **Proactive Insights:** An "Auto-Feed" that surfaces hidden patterns as soon as you upload a file.

### 🛠️ The Tech Stack:
- **Orchestration:** LangGraph (for complex analytical workflows)
- **UI:** Streamlit (Customized with Inter typography & premium CSS)
- **Engine:** FastAPI + Pandas + Plotly
- **LLM:** Groq (Llama 3 for lightning-fast reasoning)
- **Retrieval:** FAISS Vector Store for PDF RAG

This project pushed the boundaries of how we interact with data, moving from manual reporting to **intelligent, conversational decision-making**.
